CYGNUS

COORDINATES RA 19h 58m 21.67s | Dec +35° 12′ 05.7″
VELOCITY 0.999c [Relativistic Limit]
DETECTION Deep Space Survey #772-B
STATUS Pre-Production // Phase I

During a routine deep-space survey, high-energy gamma-ray bursts were identified originating from the Cygnus constellation. Initial analysis suggested a natural pulsar event. Further investigation revealed a terrifying reality: an ultra-relativistic jet, precisely aimed at Sol, moving at 99.9% the speed of light.

CYGNUS is a hard sci-fi exploration into the physics of Type III civilizations and the logistical horror of a high-velocity impact event.
